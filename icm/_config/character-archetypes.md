## Cast Rule

Use 5 active non-Skelly characters for the main demo cast. Skelly is an easter
egg, but the demo guarantees he moves in after day one.

## Personality Generation

Each non-Skelly character is dealt:

- one core temperament card
- one economic pressure card
- one social style card
- one housing preference card
- one flaw or stressor card

The agent can evolve from memories and reflections, but initial persona comes
from these cards plus sprite archetype constraints.

## Skelly

Skelly is always male and always named Skelly.

Inject these modifiers into his agent instance:

- comedically grumpy
- makes bone puns
- dislikes noisy neighbors
- oddly responsible about rent
- reacts to dramatic events with deadpan fatalism

Keep him a tenant like anyone else. Do not make him omniscient or magical in the
simulation rules.
