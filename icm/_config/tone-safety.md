# Tone And Safety

## Tone

Default tone is a professional property-management simulator with a small amount
of PG-13 dark comedy.

Incidents can be awkward, petty, stressful, or funny. The investment and
operational consequences should stay legible.

## Boundaries

- Avoid graphic violence.
- Avoid protected-class abuse or stereotypes.
- Avoid making illegal or abusive landlord behavior look consequence-free.
- If the landlord behaves badly, reflect consequences through reputation,
  complaints, churn, fines, legal risk, or reduced ROI.

## Demo Assumption

For the hackathon demo, assume the presenter acts as a decent landlord.
Full input moderation is post-hackathon scope unless explicitly requested.
