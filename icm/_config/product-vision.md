# Product Vision

## One-line Pitch

A Decision Intelligence simulator that shows how resident life, landlord
choices, property reputation, and ROI influence each other over time.

## Primary User

The demo user is a landlord or property operator evaluating how management
choices affect a building's business value and resident experience.

## Demo Shape

- Start with one configurable building.
- Seed a small cast: about 8 active characters, including residents and
  potential move-ins.
- Let resident agents propose actions based on personality, goals, needs, and
  current state.
- Let the game master adjudicate effects on story, reputation, occupancy,
  satisfaction, maintenance burden, and ROI placeholder metrics.
- Show the result through a fixed-camera Phaser panel, chat bubbles, event log,
  and landlord controls.

## Challenge Fit

This is submitted as Decision Intelligence: it helps a decision-maker test
property-management scenarios across community, investment, and operational
signals.
